### A few thoughts on Mind Uploading
- year: 2022
- kicker: Essay
- meta: A skeptical-but-curious take on identity, continuity, substrate, and what counts as the same person.
- links: [Read](./blog_posts/mind-uploading.html) [Slides](https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.8868782.v1)
The essay asks what a successful upload would even mean, how much of identity has to be preserved, and whether approximation or emulation gives a more realistic way of thinking about artificial continuations of a person.
